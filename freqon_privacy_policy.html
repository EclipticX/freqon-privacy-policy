Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 25, 2026

By using this app (“FreqOn Radio”), you agree to our privacy policy. We are committed to keeping your personal information secure and using it properly when necessary.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Country (for content localization)
  • IP address (used to suggest stations based on your current location)

Usage Information:

  • Listening history
  • Favorite stations
  • Preferred categories

2. How We Use the Information

  • To provide radio streaming and station browsing features
  • To suggest stations based on your country or IP location
  • To save your favorites and preferences
  • To send notifications and important updates
  • To improve app performance and user experience

3. Information Sharing

We may share your information only in the following cases:

  • When required by law or legal authorities
  • With authorized service providers (such as analytics or notification services)

We never sell or transfer your personal information to unauthorized third parties.

4. Data Security

  • All information is stored in secure and encrypted formats.
  • We use security measures to prevent unauthorized access.
  • You are responsible for keeping your password and sensitive data confidential.

5. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction of incorrect data
  • Request deletion of your account

6. Account Deletion

Users have the right to permanently delete their account at any time from within the app settings.

  • Once a deletion request is confirmed, the user account will be permanently removed from our database.
  • All associated personal data including name, email, preferences, and listening history will be deleted.
  • This action is irreversible and cannot be undone.

If you are unable to delete your account from the app, you may contact us at: support@freqonradio.app

7. Cookies & Tracking

We use limited cookies and analytics tools to improve user experience.
